    Tutorials

    On Pedity Platform, you are commiting all actions on blockchain. When you Login to the platform, you sign in using your Public Key but you need private key when you are creating a post, modifying your profile or setting up goals or tipping or promoting content.

    At Pedity, we don't store your private key on any server or not even on your browser. You use your private key only when you are submitting a transaction.

    Basic

    Basic tutorials such as creating wallet, creating content, setting up profile, creating fundraiser.

    Creating a Wallet

    Creating a Wallet is Simple.

    1. Click on Login/Generate Wallet
    2. Click on Generate New Wallet on new page
    3. Your new wallet is now generated, click copy button to copy your keys

    NOTE: Even though your wallet is generated, it doesn't exist unless you fund it with XLM. Make sure you send 2.5 XLM to publick key / Wallet address

    Logging in Pedity

    Logging on Pedity Platform requires your wallet to have minimum 2.5 XLM. You need PEDI tokens for certain actions but not for login.

    1. Click on Login/Generate Wallet
    2. Paste your Public Key or Wallet ID in Enter Public Key and click Authenticate

    NOTE: If you are unable to login, make sure you have funded your wallet with minimum 2.5 XLM

    Setting Your Wallet/Profile

    Using Pedity Platform, you can attach profile information in your wallet. Your profile will look like this

    1. Click on the cogs icon on the topbar. (Make sure you are logged into Pedity)
    2. Fill the Edit Profile section with your details and enter Secret Key in key secton.
    3. Click on Submit and your profile will now have details and your wallet is now converted to a social profile.

    Creating Article

    Creating an article on Pedity means that you are creating content on Stellar Blockchain.

    1. Click on the pen button on topbar. (Make sure you are logged into Pedity)
    2. Fill out Article Name, Category, Banner, Article Content and TL;DR section
    3. Click on Create Button after you have filled the details
    4. This will open a Popup where you can Create Article using your Private Key or Copy the XDR to submit article later.
    5. Click Create and you will be redirected to your article once it is successfully created.

    Tipping

    Tipping an author means sending funds to the author as a gratitude for your content.

    1. Click on the tip button on article page
    2. This will open a Popup ,fill out private key and select tip amount, asset that you wish to tip
    3. Click Tip and the author will be tipped successfully.

    Your tip will be sent successfully.

    Promoting Content

    Promoting an article means promoting a content on top of pedity platform..

    1. Click on the Promote button on article page
    2. This will open a Popup ,fill out private key
    3. Click Promote and the article will be promoted successfully.

    Article will be promoted successfully.

    Promoting an article costs a fees of 10 PEDI

    Setting Goal/Fundraiser

    Using Pedity Platform, you can set a goal and create a fundraiser campaign to raise funds.

    1. Click on the cogs icon on the topbar. (Make sure you are logged into Pedity)
    2. Fill the Edit Goal section with your details and enter Secret Key in key secton.
    3. Click on Submit and your goal will now be successfully created.

    Advanced

    Grabbing transaction id

    Your content on pedity.com (or any other node) will be like Pedity.com/article/73.......dc4062defb01

    As you might have guessed, the 73....dc4062defb01 is the transaction id. Copy this transaction id and check it on any of the block explorer, it should be a valid transaction.

    Submitting transaction to Stellar Laboratory

    On Pedity Platform, you can copy the XDR of transaction and submit the transaction to block explorer using Stellar Laboratory.

    1. Click on the pen button on topbar. (Make sure you are logged into Pedity)
    2. Fill out Article Name, Category, Banner, Article Content and TL;DR section
    3. Click on Create Button after you have filled the details
    4. This will open a Popup, Copy the XDR of the transacton
    5. Go to Stellar Labs and paste the XDR and than Import Transaction
    6. Final step is to enter the private key and submit transaction.

    Grabbing IPFS content

    Click on the Geek Stats button on article and you can find the IPFS hash in the popup
